About Us

Gateway Brewery is a casual restaurant that prides itself on using only the freshest ingredients as well as supporting local farmers by sourcing ingredients from the St. Louis area.

From the beginning, our founders wanted to foster a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere that included fresh and locally sourced ingredients to create a one-of-a-kind experience in the St. Louis area. Thanks to the support of family, friends, and others in the restaurant business, we have been able to make this dream come true.

We here at Gateway Brewery are blown away by the love and support that we have been shown and hope to serve you and St. Louis as a whole for years to come!
